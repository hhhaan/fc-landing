## Pro

Core roast-floor tools — live roast, batch history, profiles, greens/blends, daily production plan. 200 batches/month, 1 seat.

## Pro+

Everything in Pro plus **unlimited batches** and commerce integrations (including roadmap items). For high volume or store sync needs.

## Enterprise

Multi-seat, custom features, on-site support (FDE), contract/invoice billing. From **$300/mo + $19 per seat** — [contact@firstcrackiscoming.com](mailto:contact@firstcrackiscoming.com)

## Free tier

After trial you can keep **20 batches/month** free. Upgrade to Pro when you need cloud limits and advanced capacity.

## Regional pricing

Display currency can follow visit region (`/?market=kr`, etc.). Checkout is handled by Polar; the currency on the checkout screen is authoritative.
