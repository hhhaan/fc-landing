Paid plans (Pro / Pro+) bill through **Polar**. Accounts use Supabase auth; subscription state shows on the First Crack web app and desktop.

## Start a subscription

1. Sign in at [firstcrackiscoming.com](https://firstcrackiscoming.com).
2. Choose Pro or Pro+ on [Plans](/en/service/plans/).
3. **Start trial** or upgrade → `/start-pro?plan=pro-monthly` / `pro-yearly` (or `pro-plus-monthly` / `pro-plus-yearly`).
4. Complete **Polar checkout**. A 14-day trial may be included.
5. Confirm Pro status and next bill date on the [account page](https://firstcrackiscoming.com/account).

When the trial ends, paid billing for the selected plan starts automatically. Cancel before the trial ends to avoid the first paid charge.

## Billing period

| Option | Notes |
|--------|-------|
| **Monthly** | Renews each month; cancel anytime |
| **Yearly** | Prepaid year; ~20% lower monthly equivalent |

Enterprise is monthly/yearly contract — [sales](mailto:contact@firstcrackiscoming.com)

## Cancel

1. Check status on the [account page](https://firstcrackiscoming.com/account).
2. Cancel or change payment method via Polar customer portal or [support@firstcrackiscoming.com](mailto:support@firstcrackiscoming.com).

**On cancel:** Pro features remain until the current period ends, then free tier (20 batches/month).

:::caution[No refunds by default]
Ending mid-period does **not** refund unused days. Fees are non-refundable except as in the [Refund policy](/en/service/refund/).
:::

## Change payment method

Update the card in the Polar portal. Ask [support@firstcrackiscoming.com](mailto:support@firstcrackiscoming.com) for a portal link if needed.

## Failed payment (past_due)

If a charge fails (limit, expiry, decline):

1. Account may show a **Payment issue** banner.
2. Update the payment method or contact support.
3. Long unpaid status cancels the subscription into free tier.

## Receipts & tax invoices

Get receipts from Polar checkout/portal. For Korean business tax invoices, email [support@firstcrackiscoming.com](mailto:support@firstcrackiscoming.com) with your business registration number.

## Enterprise & invoices

Contract, bank transfer, and invoice billing are Enterprise-only. [contact@firstcrackiscoming.com](mailto:contact@firstcrackiscoming.com)
